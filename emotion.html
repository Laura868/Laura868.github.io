One emotion for each day.

Personal Project

I began with the concept of human emotions, and to give shape to this idea I decided to work with abstract photographs suggesting emotions captured on postcards.

I wish to give people "one emotion for each day", not a conditioned emotion but one that depends on the viewer's mood, impacting on what the image on the card evokes. I only added a word on each card, in order for the emotion to be a positive one.

I thought these cards needed a place to be kept, and this place at the same time could be part of the project. The idea was to make a box, similar to the ones used in casinos, where cards come out one by one. In this way, I could play with the viewer's curiosity.

The box's system works by turning a handle, producing then a card through a small opening. Spectators would then have an emotion in their hands, or rather a way to activate an emotion.
