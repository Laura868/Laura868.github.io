Panasonic

A4 Poster for an established company

This is a hypothetical task where I will showcase my ability to work with an established brand and translate their brand into my design.

I chose the brand Panasonic. The product I decided to work with is a camera, able to undergo extreme conditions, waterproof and resisting low temperatures, crashes...

To convey this idea, I started working with the concept of "resistant to extreme environments". I then searched for a photo of an extreme environment, an underwater London, in which I saw a perfect match for what I was looking for. I then left just the square camera focused, to convey how the camera continues to operate despite the conditions, making its strength clear.

Throughout the process, I always had in mind Panasonic's corporate concept, in order to create a graphic in line with the existing brand.
