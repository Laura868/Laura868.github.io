Home Staging

Brief

Design a campaign for David Phillips Home Staging accross print and digital platforms.

The campaign is to promote the home staging services for the next 12-18 months, focused in the high-end luxury side of this service. With the aim to attrack developers, estate agencies and particular clients as well.

Target Audience: High-end developers, estate agencies and particular clients who want to sell/rent their properties as well as maximise value and reduce sale time. Aim: Drive the audience to use DPHS.

Home Staging

Home Staging Campaign

For this project I worked mainly with the idea of a grid made of triangles which resemble the sketch of a house.

This grid is made of white fine lines, trying to communicate elegance to our target high-end clients.

This concept also contains a slogan which communicates effectiveness and, once more, that this is the company that they are looking for.

I decided to develop this idea because I thought it was more up to date and versatile than anothers I had.
