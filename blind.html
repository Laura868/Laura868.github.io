Blindness

Book Cover Redesign

Blindness is meant as a wake up call, as a portrait of the world as it is now, a nudge to truly accept the world as we see it around us and to act accordingly.

I really like this book and although I think the current book design cover is brilliant I wanted to create another design based on illustration instead of based on typography, just another point of view.

Conveying the idea of confusion in a society immersed in a “blindness epidemic” and the only woman able to see in the middle of the chaos.
