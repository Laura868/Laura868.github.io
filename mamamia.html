Advertising graphics

I am trying to present an eclectic design inspired by both classic and modern.

The classic half is rooted in the history of Italy, where the pizzeria is located, with a Renaissance-inspired ornamentation and Romanic typography.

On the other hand, there is a modern arrangement of the elements, its aligment and Riot Squad typography, with the distinctive features of a modern typeface.This typography, similar to Bodoni, gives the design character and elegance.

In conclusion: a graphic that walks between the past and the present, that fits in a consistent manner in the time we live in.
